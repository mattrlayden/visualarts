My art journey began in high school when I decided to take a photography as a sort of fun class. Before then I had not been too much of an art fellow. I focused primarily on tennis, my favorite sport to play, school, and hanging out with my friends. I started talking with my camera with me every time that I walked my dog and was amazed by the different pictures, I could make simply by changing a couple of settings.

I picked up drawing as a sort of hobby during my freshman year. When signing up for my class I didn’t think that I was doing too much to encourage myself to be creative. Because of this I decided to sign up for an introductory art and drawing class. Anyway, while in the class I found it to be a really good way to relax and enjoyed it a lot. Because of this experience I continue to practice drawing to this day.
